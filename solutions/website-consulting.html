Website consulting is a part of our business we are extremely passionate about. As experts in the field of web design and development, we have a passion of educating businesses and individuals what it takes to run a successful website. Website consulting is a way we like to start off our relationship with potential clients. No only do we want to tell you how your site can do better we also like to show how we're going to make it better.

When we do our website consulting we go over the following topics:

  • On-Page Search Engine Optimization Opportunities
  • Improved User Experience Opportunities
  • User Interface Opportunities
  • Marketing Opportunities
  • Content Opportunities
  • Design Improvements
  • and More!

We'll help answer questions such as:

  • Why am I not showing up in Google?
  • Why am i not getting contacted from my online contact form?
  • Why do my customer never mention they saw my website?
  • Why does my competitor rank better than I do?
  • What can I do to increase the visibility of my brand?
  • Is the information what my customers are looking for?
  • and More!

As website consultants we aim to answer the confusing questions and decode terms such as SEO, PPC, SEM, AdWords, SERP, and other industry lingo made to baffle those who don't understand the web. Our goal is to make you comfortable with your digital assets and help you understand how to use them to maximize your ROI. No more will you be put off by not being able to understand the web. Our website consulting solution also goes great with our website training solutions.
