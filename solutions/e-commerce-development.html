Secure, User-Friendly, & SEO Friendly Online Stores

We have offered Houston e-commerce development solutions since 2010 for companies around the Houston Area. E-commerce sites need to build traffic and turn visitors into customers quickly in order to become successful. Although clients tend to choose us for custom e-commerce sites that need to address specific business goals, such as mobile accessibility, B2B custom features, and increasing conversion rates, we always start with the fundamentals of e-commerce web design principles & security.

Product Catalog

Compelling and organized

The design of your product pages will affect your sales. The more images and the more information you display about each product the better your product will sell, especially when presented in an easy-to-use design. But managing this extra data efficiently requires an easy-to-use content management tools like WordPress and Magento Commerce. A pleasant user experience based design and robust content management tool allow you to include:

  • Faceted catalog filtering
  • Multiple product images and zoom features
  • Multiple varieties and options with images and prices
  • Tabbed content: product suggestions, customer reviews
  • Multiple prices: MSRP, sale, clearance, quantity discounts
  • Custom rules: "out of stock" messages

Designed for your customers and your business
